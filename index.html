The Foundation

The Art of Prompting Your Way to Growth

Before diving into the libraries, understand the mindset shift. AI is not a search engine. It's a mirror, a sparring partner, and a creativity amplifier — when you know how to talk to it.

Give It a Role

Start every growth prompt with "Act as a…" — Stoic philosopher, CBT therapist, life coach, nutritionist. The more specific the role, the more powerful the response.

Provide Context

Share your goals, struggles, and constraints. The more the AI knows about you, the more personal and actionable its guidance becomes. Treat it like a real conversation.

Ask for Challenges

Don't just ask for information — ask to be questioned, challenged, and pushed. "Question my assumptions" or "Find the flaw in my thinking" unlocks real growth.

Iterate and Deepen

The magic happens in the follow-up. Respond to what AI gives you. Push back. Ask it to go deeper. A 10-message exchange is worth 10 generic prompts.

Build a Memory

Create a "Personal Context Document" — your goals, values, challenges — and paste it at the start of each session. Instant personalisation, every time.

Save What Works

Keep a prompt journal. When a prompt produces a breakthrough, save it. Your personal library of prompts becomes one of your most valuable growth assets.

The Prompt Library

Organised by life domain. Copy any prompt and paste it directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI assistant. Fill in the [brackets] with your details.

The Socratic Questioner

"Act as a Socratic philosopher. I believe [limiting belief]. Question me relentlessly to expose the flaws in this belief and help me arrive at a more empowering truth."

💡 Best for: Breaking through self-limiting beliefs

Gratitude Amplifier

"I want to practice gratitude. Ask me five deep questions that uncover hidden blessings in my day, then help me write a gratitude list in my own voice."

💡 Best for: Daily gratitude practice, shifting perspective

Future Self Visualization

"Guide me through a vivid 5-minute visualization of my ideal future self in [timeframe]. Describe the scene, my emotions, my environment, and my accomplishments. Then ask what three steps I can take today to move toward that vision."

💡 Best for: Goal-setting, motivation, manifestation

The Stoic Journal

"Act as Marcus Aurelius. I'm facing [challenge]. Respond to me in the style of Meditations — direct, philosophical, and compassionate. Then ask me what a virtuous person would do in my situation."

💡 Best for: Navigating challenges with ancient wisdom

Affirmation Architect

"I want to rewire my self-image around [goal or quality]. Create 10 powerful, present-tense affirmations that feel authentic to me, not generic. Make them emotionally resonant and specific to someone who is [brief description of yourself]."

💡 Best for: Building new identity, confidence

Shadow Work Guide

"Act as a Jungian therapist. Help me explore my shadow self. Ask me 5 questions about patterns or reactions in my life that frustrate me, then help me see what unacknowledged part of myself might be behind them."

💡 Best for: Deep self-awareness, emotional healing

Custom Yoga Sequence

"Design a 20-minute yoga sequence for someone who is [fitness level] and wants to [goal: open hips / build strength / wind down for sleep]. Include pose names, duration, and transition cues."

💡 Best for: Personalised home practice

Recovery Optimizer

"My HRV today is [number] and I slept [hours]. My readiness score is [score]. Based on this data, recommend whether I should train hard, go easy, or rest today — and explain the science behind your recommendation."

💡 Best for: Biometric-informed training decisions

Ancient Movement Wisdom

"What did [Stoics / ancient Greeks / Ayurvedic tradition] believe about the relationship between physical movement and mental health? Summarise the key principles, then suggest 3 daily movement practices based on that wisdom."

💡 Best for: Connecting movement to philosophy

Body Scan Debrief

"I just finished a body scan and noticed [sensations / areas of tension]. Act as a somatic therapist and help me understand what these physical sensations might be communicating emotionally. Then suggest one movement or breath practice to address it."

💡 Best for: Mind-body connection, somatic healing

Guided Meditation Script

"Write a 10-minute guided meditation script for someone who is feeling [emotion] and wants to feel [desired state]. Use nature imagery, slow pacing, and breathing cues. End with an intention-setting moment."

💡 Best for: Custom meditations for any state

Breathwork Prescription

"I'm experiencing [anxiety / low energy / racing thoughts / poor sleep]. Recommend the single best breathwork technique for my situation, explain the neuroscience behind why it works, and give me step-by-step instructions."

💡 Best for: Targeted breathwork selection

Zen Koan Contemplation

"Give me a Zen koan appropriate for someone focused on [theme: ego dissolution / presence / impermanence]. Then guide me through a 5-minute contemplation exercise and ask me what arises."

💡 Best for: Deep mindfulness, non-dual awareness

"Design a 15-minute evening wind-down ritual that incorporates mindfulness, gentle movement, and reflection. Base it on [Ayurvedic / Buddhist / Stoic] evening traditions and adapt it for a modern person who [constraint: has kids / works late / is stressed]."

Ikigai Excavator

💡 Best for: Finding life purpose, career clarity

The 80-Year-Old Test

"Act as my 80-year-old self looking back on my life. I'm currently facing this decision: [decision]. What do you wish I had done? What would have mattered? What would have been a waste of time? Be brutally honest."

💡 Best for: Big life decisions, regret minimisation

Personal Values Audit

"Help me clarify my top 5 core values. Ask me a series of questions about moments when I felt most alive, most frustrated, and most proud. Then identify the values underlying each response and help me rank them."

💡 Best for: Values clarification, authentic living

The Aristotle Goal Audit

"I want to achieve [goal]. Act as Aristotle and help me examine whether this goal leads to eudaimonia — true flourishing. Is this goal aligned with my deepest nature? What virtues will it require and develop? What obstacles should I anticipate?"

💡 Best for: Goal alignment with deeper values

Empathy Expander

💡 Best for: Conflict resolution, empathy building

Communication Coach

"I need to have a difficult conversation with [person] about [topic]. Coach me using Nonviolent Communication (NVC). Help me identify my observations, feelings, needs, and requests. Then roleplay as the other person so I can practice."

💡 Best for: Difficult conversations, NVC practice

Love Languages Decoder

"Help me conduct an energy audit of my week. I'll list my main activities. For each one, ask me whether it gives or drains energy, then help me redesign my schedule to protect my most energising activities and minimise energy vampires."

💡 Best for: Sustainable productivity, burnout prevention

Deep Work Designer

Decision Detox

Morning Ritual Builder

"Design a morning ritual for me using wisdom from Stoicism, Ayurveda, and modern performance science. I have [X minutes] and my biggest challenge is [challenge]. Prioritise practices with the highest return on time invested."

💡 Best for: Morning routine, peak performance
