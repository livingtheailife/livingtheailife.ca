AI Prompt Library

Wellness Prompts for ChatGPT

Copy these prompts directly into ChatGPT, Claude, or any AI assistant to unlock powerful wellness support.

Morning Mindset

Act as a Stoic philosopher and compassionate life coach. I want to start my day with clarity and purpose. Ask me three questions about my values and goals for today, then generate a personalized morning affirmation based on my answers. Keep it grounded, not fluffy.

Evening Reflection

Guide me through a structured evening reflection using the Stoic practice of negative visualization and gratitude. Ask me what I accomplished today, what I could have done better, and what I am grateful for. Then summarize my reflection and suggest one focus for tomorrow.

Meal Planning

Create a 7-day anti-inflammatory meal plan for me based on Mediterranean and Ayurvedic principles. I'm allergic to [X]. Include breakfast, lunch, dinner, and one snack per day. For each meal, briefly explain its primary health benefit.

Breathwork Design

Design a personalized 10-minute breathwork sequence for reducing anxiety and entering a focused state. Explain each phase, how long to hold each part of the breath, and the physiological reason it helps. Format it so I can follow it in real time.

Limiting Belief Work

Act as a CBT therapist. I'm going to share a limiting belief I have about myself. Ask me Socratic questions to challenge the belief, find counter-evidence, and help me reframe it into an empowering alternative perspective. My belief: [insert belief].

Meditation Script

Write a 10-minute guided meditation script for body scan relaxation. Use a calm, unhurried tone. Begin with three deep breaths, move systematically from feet to crown, include a visualization of healing light, and end with a gentle return to waking awareness.
